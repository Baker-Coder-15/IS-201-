How to Get Started with Tennis

  1. Get Your Gear
    • Racquet that matches your level
    • Comfortable tennis shoes
    • New tennis balls
  2. Learn the Basics
    • Forehand and backhand grip
    • Ready position and footwork
    • Simple serve motion
  3. Practice with Purpose
    • Consistency over power
    • Track your sessions
    • Play with better players when possible

My Favorite Tennis Drills

Baseline Consistency

Rally cross-court and try to get 20 balls in a row without missing. Focus on height over the net and clean footwork.

Serve Targets

Place cones in each service box and aim to hit each cone area 5 times before moving on. This builds accuracy and confidence.

Approach & Volley

Have a partner feed short balls, attack the net, and finish with controlled volleys. Work on staying balanced through contact.
