About Jake

BYU Finance Student | Functional Fitness & Meal Prep

Who I Am

I’m a finance student at Brigham Young University (Class of 2028) who loves training, meal prep, and building systems that make a healthy lifestyle realistic for busy people. Over the last six years of lifting, I’ve gained around 30 pounds of healthy muscle and fallen in love with functional fitness, mobility work, and the mental side of training.

I’m an outdoorsman at heart—I feel closest to God when I’m moving my body, pushing my limits, and being present in nature. I love the grind, but I also love seeing other people win. Whether it’s helping a friend build a basic lifting routine, cut weight in a healthy way, or simply feel more confident in their body, I’m all in on growth.

This site is a simple hub for functional workouts, practical meal prep ideas, and tools that make it easier to take a chance on yourself physically, mentally, and spiritually.
